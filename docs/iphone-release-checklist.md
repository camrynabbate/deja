# 10-minute iPhone check

Use a disposable test account and the exact build planned for the App Store. Mark every item Pass or Fail.

## Feed

- Scroll continuously for 30 seconds. The feed does not jump, freeze, or refresh by itself.
- Pull down while partway through the feed. Nothing refreshes.
- Return to the top and pull down. The feed refreshes once.
- Change category, price, and color filters. Results update immediately.

## Products and saved items

- Tap a product once. Its details open.
- Double-tap a product. It becomes liked without opening.
- Add and remove a like and a save.
- Open the Saved tab and open a saved product.
- Leave and reopen the feed. Recently viewed products remain.
- Tap Shop. Only the expected retailer page opens.

## Photo search

- Take a photo and confirm results appear.
- Choose an unsupported or very large image and confirm a clear message appears.
- Repeat several searches and confirm the app eventually asks you to try again later.

## Styleboards

- Create a board and open it.
- Tap two saved products to add them.
- Move, resize, and remove an item using touch.
- Save, leave the board, and reopen it. The layout remains.

## Account and recovery

- Force-close and reopen the app. It returns to a usable screen.
- With the disposable account, clear all data and confirm saved items and boards disappear.
- Delete the disposable account and confirm it can no longer sign in.

## Release decision

Do not upload the build if scrolling, account deletion, photo search, or styleboard saving fails. Record the phone model, iOS version, and failed step.
